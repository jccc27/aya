About Us

Maranatha Christian Academy, Calamba Chapter, is a distinguished private Christian school located in Calamba, Laguna. Part of the extensive MCA network, the Calamba chapter is dedicated to providing high-quality education from preschool to high school. Emphasizing both academic excellence and Christian values, MCA Calamba nurtures students to become well-rounded individuals equipped with knowledge, character, and faith. The school fosters a supportive and spiritually enriching environment, preparing students for both academic success and meaningful lives rooted in Christian principles..
